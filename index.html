About UBC Launch Pad

Engineering Design Student Teams at UBC consist of technically diverse students of all faculties who team up to build challenging year-long projects.

UBC Launch Pad aims to build exciting web or mobile applications from start to finish. From the initial market research, to full-stack hands-on development, to the deployment and marketing of the final product. This can be a great opportunity to develop new skills and build upon existing ones in an agile and scrum team environment.
