Launch Pad is UBC's one and only student-run software incubator.
Our club implements software projects in a collaborative and
professional environment.

About Us

We aim to be the leading organization on campus for students to learn and work on innovative projects and technologies.

Our multiple platform teams work together on original ideas from conceptualization to release.
Over the the past year, we've grown our development team from four to over forty members, hosted a coding workshop, and developed multiple test applications.

PLATFORM TEAMS

Let's make the next big app and come join our platform specific teams. We're currently working on iOS, Android, Node, and Django apps.

MOONSHOT

Our resident R&D division, our Moonshot Team is responsible for exploring cutting edge technologies. Think artificial intelligence, IoT, big data.

CODING WORKSHOPS

Our first coding workshop, Tech 101, was incredibly successful. We're looking to do many, many more in the coming months.
