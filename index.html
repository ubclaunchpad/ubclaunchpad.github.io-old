Launch Pad is UBC's student-run software development club.
Our club implements software projects in a collaborative and
professional environment.

About Us

We aim to be the leading organization on campus for students to develop their software development skills by collaborating on fun and interesting projects.

PLATFORM TEAMS

Our platform teams dive deep into their platform of choice and develop apps from conception to release. We currently have JavaScript, Ruby, iOS, Android, and Machine Learning teams.

MOONSHOT

Our moonshot team explores cutting-edge technologies and tackles challenging problems. Think IoT, distributed computing, AI.

WORKSHOPS

We host interview workshops and tech talks throughout the school year to connect with the UBC tech community and increase interest in software development among UBC students.

Launch Pad has big plans for the upcoming year. Are you a hustler looking to impact the tech community at UBC and Vancouver? Or a coder looking for the opportunity to work on awesome projects with cool people? Join our team!


Platform Teams


Description

Requirements

  • Passionate about building great software

  • Can attend meetings every Saturday 12-4PM
