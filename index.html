Launch Pad is UBC's one and only student-run software development club.
Our club implements software projects in a collaborative and
professional environment.

About Us

We aim to be the leading organization on campus for students to learn and work on innovative projects and technologies.

Our multiple platform teams work together on original ideas from conceptualization to release.
Over the the past year, we've grown our development team from four to over forty members, hosted a coding workshop, and developed multiple test applications.


PLATFORM TEAMS

Let's make the next big app and come join our platform specific teams. We're currently working on iOS, Android, Node, and Django apps.

CODING WORKSHOPS

Our first coding workshop, Tech 101, was incredibly successful. We're looking to do many, many more in the coming months.

Join Us

Launch Pad has big plans for the upcoming new year, and we're looking for members to join our development and business teams. Are you a hustler looking to impact the tech community at UBC and Vancouver? Or a coder looking for the opportunity to work in a team? Join our team!

Vice President of External Affairs


Description

  • Seeks new sponsors

  • Maintains healthy relations with current sponsors

  • Establishes partnerships with businesses and on-campus clubs

  • Ensures sponsorship commitments are executed effectively and on time


Requirements

  • Strong communication and teamwork skills

  • Excellent written skills

  • Nice to have: Previous sponsorship or corporate partnership experience

Vice President of Marketing


Description

  • Promotes and maintains the Launch Pad brand on campus and across Vancouver

  • Develops marketing strategies for Launch Pad

  • Event planning and marketing

  • Manages Launch Pad’s on-line presence though social media, our website, and mailing list emails

  • Oversees creative director and web developer


Requirements

  • People person

  • Previous marketing experience

  • Excellent written skills

  • Nice to have: HTML/CSS/JavaScript
