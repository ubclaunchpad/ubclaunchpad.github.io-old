About Us


We aim to be the leading organization on campus for students to learn and work on innovative projects and technologies.

Our multiple platform teams work together on original ideas from conceptualization to release. Over the the past year, we've grown our development team from four to over forty members, hosted a coding workshop, and developed multiple test applications.
