About Us

In September 2015, Launch Pad was founded upon the mission of creating one web/mobile app to be released onto the app store. We wanted to inspire our community and create something brand new and innovative. However, as we engaged in the tech community, we realized there was a bigger problem: we saw a huge void between entrepreneurs and developers. Why are entreprenuers outsourcing their software development while there are motivated students able to do the work better? We therefore pivoted ourselves with the goal of connecting ideas with developers while still inspiring the community about tech.

Launch Pad’s team of motivated student developers build engaging web and mobile apps for our growing clientele whom are managed by our in-house business analysts. We aim to connect eager students with real experiences that they may benefit from, financially and intellectually, while completing their studies. Our management team drives professional sales with clients, delivering products on-time and on-budget. Interested in working with us? Pitch us your product and we’ll do if for a cost-optimized price while maximizing the value for our developers. Email us at business@ubclaunchpad.com
