About Us

Officially founded in September 2015, Launch Pad originally sought to select one App to develop over the course of the year to be released by the end of year in April. However, as our interactions with the tech community grew we realised the shortcomings in the status quo regarding:

  • Knowledge of the costs of technology throughout traditional industries
  • Knowledge gaps in the benefits of technology in traditional industries
  • The high barriers of entry to simply build out MVP’s for new start-ups
  • The large rift that exists between business and developers, in terms of communication and community

Having discovered these walls within the industry itself, we realised that our mission had to change to benefit the community in general.

Now, we seek to create community value by developing technology to fit enterprise, professional, and personal needs, by the community for the community. Our ultimate vision is to become the premier authority on technology with at UBC and beyond, improve software practises, educate students inspire trends to reduce barriers to entry for start-up culture.
